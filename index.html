About

Trusted Advisor to the Leaders
Who Drive Transformation.

I’ve spent 22 years at the intersection of technology strategy and executive talent — partnering with global, national, and PE-backed organizations to identify and place the leaders who modernize platforms, advance data and AI capabilities, and drive measurable outcomes.

My focus is on positions where technology, commercial leadership, and customer strategy converge — from President and General Manager of a technology business to CIO, CTO, CDO, Head of AI, and VP of Engineering. I operate across Technology, Financial Services, Consumer, Industrial, and Life Sciences.

I began my career in enterprise software consulting, building early relationships with organizations like Adobe, OpenText, and Microsoft. That practitioner’s perspective informs every mandate I take on. Over the last 11 years my work has been exclusively executive search — VP to C-suite, retained engagements across North America.
